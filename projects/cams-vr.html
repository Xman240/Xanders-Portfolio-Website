CAMS VR Medical Simulation

A VR medical simulation proof of concept built for the Center of Advanced Medical Simulation at NAIT.

← Back to Projects Demo / Repo Link

Overview

My NAIT Capstone project involved helping build a proof of concept for a medical simulation in VR. The project used a digital twin of the client facility and an AI chatbot plugin to support immersive, dynamic conversation.

Technical Focus

Unreal Engine, VR interaction constraints, AI chatbot integration, and working with client-focused requirements.

Challenge

The challenge was creating a believable proof of concept that could support open-ended conversation in a VR medical setting.

What I Learned

I gained experience working with Unreal Engine, VR simulation expectations, team project constraints, and client-driven feature goals.
