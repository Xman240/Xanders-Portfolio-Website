CAMS VR Medical Simulation

A NAIT capstone proof of concept for immersive VR medical simulation.

← Back to ProjectsDemo / Repo Link

Overview

This project was built for the Center of Advanced Medical Simulation at NAIT. The goal was to create a realistic human model that could engage in immersive conversations inside a digital twin of their facility.

My Role

I helped build the VR proof of concept using Unreal Engine and supported the implementation of dynamic AI-driven conversation tools.

Challenge

The main challenge was creating a believable simulation experience while working within VR, AI plugin, and client-driven requirements.

What I Learned

I learned more about Unreal Engine, VR development, client communication, and how simulation projects need to balance realism with technical constraints.
